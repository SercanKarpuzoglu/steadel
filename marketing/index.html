01What Steadel does

Three quiet automations that guard your margins.

⏸️

Stock-aware ads guard

When a tracked product sells out, its linked Meta ad sets are paused automatically — and resumed when it's back in stock. Steadel never touches campaigns a human paused.

🔔

Low-stock alerts

Set a threshold per product or store-wide. The moment inventory crosses it, you get an email (or Slack message) — before the product page goes dark.

📬

Scheduled reports

A calm daily or weekly digest of inventory movement and alerts, delivered to your inbox. Know the state of your store without logging in.

05FAQ

Fair questions.

Which platforms does Steadel support?

Shopify and WooCommerce today. The ads guard connects to Meta (Facebook/Instagram) ads; Google Ads is on the roadmap.

Does Steadel need access to my customers' data?

No. We request read access to products and inventory only. Customer data never reaches our servers — and that's verifiable in the OAuth consent screen when you connect.

What happens when a product is restocked?

Ad sets that Steadel paused are resumed automatically once stock crosses your threshold again. Campaigns paused by a person stay paused — we never override human decisions.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, self-serve from the billing page. Access continues until the end of the paid period. You can export all your data as JSON before you go.

How is support handled?

In writing, by e-mail — answered by the people who build Steadel, within one business day. No call centers, no chatbots.
